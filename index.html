I was born in Milwaukee Wi. and lived there until I was 11 when my parents decided to move to Phoenix AZ. I am glad they did, because I enjoyed growing up in the desert where the wheather was good all year around, although very hot in the summer!

I loved riding dirt bikes in the desert and even got into racing for awhile. I also love the water and spent alot of time at the local lakes swimming and riding jet skis. Later my love of the water got me into scuba diving where I became an instructor.

Now, my wife and I live on the beach in Mexico and go diving, surfing or just hanging out in the water when we are not working or looking for the next best taco joint. Life can take you to places you never thought you would go if you just let it!
